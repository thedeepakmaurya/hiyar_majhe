About Hiyar Majhe

Revered across the world, Rabindranath Tagore continues to inspire awe through his vision, creativity, and deep connection with nature and humanity. Hiyar Majhe brings this vision to life in the heart of Shantiniketan, offering you a unique opportunity to live amidst the poetic beauty of Ballavpur—just moments away from the serene River Kopai and the culturally rich Amar Kutir Society.

Spread across approximately 3 acres, Hiyar Majhe is a thoughtfully planned residential complex featuring luxurious 2 BHK, 3 BHK, and 4 BHK duplex bungalows, along with elegantly designed studio apartments. Developed to reflect the charm and soul of Tagore’s land, the project blends modern comfort with the peace of rural living.

Amenities include a serene swimming pool, clubhouse, guest house, landscaped gardens, decorative water bodies, fountains, and multipurpose courts—designed to enrich your lifestyle while maintaining harmony with nature. With round-the-clock security and CCTV surveillance, you can enjoy your days here with complete peace of mind.

Hiyar Majhe is not just a place to live—it’s an experience shaped by Tagore’s spirit, where modern luxury meets timeless tranquility.

Mission

  • To offer a soulful living experience that reflects the poetic spirit of Shantiniketan and the legacy of Rabindranath Tagore
  • To create thoughtfully designed bungalow communities that seamlessly blend luxury, nature, and cultural heritage
  • To ensure every resident enjoys comfort, peace, and a deep connection to the serene surroundings of Ballavpur
  • To uphold transparency, quality, and client-centric values throughout the ownership journey.

Vision

  • To be a trusted name in boutique bungalow and eco-living development in culturally rich destinations
  • To craft lifestyle spaces that honour nature, art, and tradition while offering modern-day comforts
  • To nurture communities that embody tranquility, beauty, and sustainable living.
  • To set new benchmarks in heritage-inspired real estate projects with lasting value for residents and investors alike.
