What makes HIYAR MAJHE special from others

  • Inspired by Tagore’s Legacy: Nestled in the heart of Shantiniketan, Hiyar Majhe is more than just a residential project—it's a soulful tribute to Rabindranath Tagore’s vision of harmonious living with nature, art, and culture.
  • Prime Location: Located in Ballavpur, just minutes from the River Kopai and Amar Kutir Society, Hiyar Majhe offers the perfect blend of natural beauty and cultural richness.
  • Luxurious Yet Rooted The project features 2, 3 & 4 BHK duplex bungalows and studio apartments that combine modern design with the peaceful charm of rural Bengal.
  • Thoughtfully Curated Amenities From landscaped gardens and decorative water bodies to a swimming pool, clubhouse, guest house, and 24x7 security—every amenity is designed to enhance your lifestyle.
  • Community-Centric Living: Hiyar Majhe promotes a sense of togetherness with spaces that encourage interaction, events, and community bonding.
  • Eco-Conscious Approach: With initiatives under the ‘Khushee’ banner, including tree plantations and support for local communities, Hiyar Majhe is committed to sustainable and socially responsible living.
  • Developer Credibility: Brought to you by Krishna Charita Eco Homes, the project is backed by a team with a strong track record of quality development and customer satisfaction.

Working Towards a Brighter Future

Hiyar Majhe is dedicated to creating a positive impact through a variety of social welfare initiatives under the ‘Khushee’ banner. From tree plantation drives and distributing clothes and blankets to the underprivileged, to arranging medical insurance for Bauls and protecting greenery during natural calamities—every effort reflects our commitment to the community and the environment.

Our initiatives include tree plantation drives to restore green cover, distribution of blankets, clothes, and daily essentials to underprivileged families, and providing medical insurance and support to the Baul community—keepers of Bengal’s rich musical legacy. We also organize efforts to protect and nurture trees during storms and other natural calamities, ensuring the environment stays safeguarded for future generations.
